title: Keeping experienced operators sharp on laws and regulations
client: NNVO
client_logo: nnvo.png
project: Laws & Regulations refresher
sector: Maritime sector and training
doelgroep: Bridge and lock operators with years of experience
vorm: Game-based online training with MicroGames, comics and discussion sheets
rol: Storyline, MicroGames and discussion sheets
summary: >-
  How do you keep experienced bridge and lock operators sharp in a field where the rules are
  constantly evolving? For NNVO, Preludens developed a refresher that challenges experience rather
  than re-teaching the basics.
highlights:
  - Challenging experience instead of repeating the basics
  - 14 learning objectives as separate, modular scenarios
  - A practice day with discussion sheets for deeper dialogue
uitdaging: >-
  Operators active for years hold a lot of experience, but rules and insights keep shifting. How do
  you refresh their core knowledge without falling back on a traditional, unchallenging refresher
  course?
ontwerpvraag: >-
  How do we challenge experienced professionals so it becomes visible where knowledge has faded and
  where reinforcement is needed — while keeping engagement high?
aanpak: >-
  In collaboration with Drift Learning (content and learning objectives), Preludens designed the
  storyline and MicroGames. Short, interactive MicroGames challenge operators to make choices under
  time pressure, with direct feedback on every mistake. Storytelling in comics translates complex
  rules into recognisable scenarios operators can relate to their work.
route: >-
  The training consists of 14 learning objectives, worked out as 14 separate scenarios with linked
  MicroGames. Thanks to the modular setup — around 15 minutes per objective, 2 to 4 hours in total
  — operators set their own pace and order via the NNVO learning platform.
ervaring: >-
  After the online training comes a practice day. Using discussion sheets — visual cards with
  scenarios and five focused questions — participants discuss in small groups the grey areas where
  rules are not clear-cut. Objectives in the morning, practical application in the afternoon: this
  creates a deep dialogue.
resultaat: >-
  After two successful pilot days, guided and tested by NNVO's trainers, the refresher has been
  refined and is ready for broader use. Experienced operators keep their knowledge current and are
  better prepared for an ever-changing field.
inzicht: >-
  The interplay between MicroGames and storytelling keeps learning dynamic and practice-oriented,
  fostering not only knowledge retention but also critical thinking and problem-solving.
