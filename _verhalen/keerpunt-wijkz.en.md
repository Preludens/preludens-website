title: A card game that opens the conversation about money worries
client: Wijkz
project: Keerpunt
sector: Welfare and social domain
jaar: 2024
doelgroep: Residents with (hidden) money worries and their support workers
vorm: Serious card game with online platform
rol: Game design, development and pilot support
summary: >-
  Together with welfare organisation Wijkz, Preludens designed the card game Keerpunt to make
  hidden poverty in The Hague discussable. A low-threshold game that encourages residents to
  share their story and seek help in time.
highlights:
  - A safe, low-threshold opening for a sensitive conversation
  - Recognisable situations and emotions from real life
  - A pilot of 700 games, scaled up to several city districts
uitdaging: >-
  Residents with money worries often wait until the very last moment to ask for help — held back
  by shame, pride and underestimating the problem. That makes starting the conversation and
  offering targeted help increasingly difficult.
ontwerpvraag: >-
  How do we break through the barrier of shame and silence, so residents recognise themselves,
  dare to share their story and find the right help in time?
aanpak: >-
  We chose a card game: the most natural, accessible form of play, easy to produce at scale. In
  Keerpunt, players seek a balance between income, energy and focus. Unforeseen events can disrupt
  that balance and pull players into a downward spiral — exactly the emotion the target group
  experiences every day.
ervaring: >-
  The strength of Keerpunt lies in real stories and examples drawn from conversations with the
  target group. Players recognise the situations and emotions, which creates room to share their
  own story. Because support workers (fictionally) end up in the same situation, roles reverse and
  a more honest, personal conversation emerges.
resultaat: >-
  Keerpunt started as a pilot with 700 games and an online platform in the Laak district, and is
  being scaled up to two more districts. A train-the-trainer programme lets support workers use the
  game independently in their daily work.
inzicht: >-
  A few well-considered rules and recognisable emotions create a safe environment in which people
  find the trust to share. The project team consisted of Preludens, Stichting Wijkz, The
  Facilitating Company and Wild Card.
