      <div class="split__text">
        <span class="section-label">Why Prepo is needed</span>
        <h2>From a one-off delivery to lasting overview</h2>
        <p>
          Many learning products start out neat: a first version, a clear link and a group of participants. But over time,
          fragmentation creeps in: new versions, feedback via email and documents, scattered files, circulating test links
          and statistics that are hard to find.
        </p>
        <p>
          Prepo prevents management from becoming a loose collection of agreements, files and links. It brings structure
          around the learning experience: which products are active, which version is in use, where feedback arrives and
          how participants reach the MicroGames or Stories.
        </p>
      </div>
