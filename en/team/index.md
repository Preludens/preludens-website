<section class="section section--navy">
  <div class="container">
    <div class="block-head">
      <span class="section-label">What we stand for</span>
      <h2>Four principles that guide every design</h2>
    </div>
    <ul class="value-list">
      <li>
        <strong>Robust</strong>
        <span>A learning experience must inspire confidence. We design solutions that feel logical, work reliably and are clear. The technology should never get in the way of the experience.</span>
      </li>
      <li>
        <strong>Purposeful</strong>
        <span>We make design choices not because they're fun, but because they contribute to learning. Every story, choice and MicroGame traces back to clear learning goals.</span>
      </li>
      <li>
        <strong>Accessible</strong>
        <span>Complex knowledge doesn't have to feel complicated. We translate content into recognisable situations, clear choices and concrete feedback, so people can discover and grow.</span>
      </li>
      <li>
        <strong>Curious</strong>
        <span>We don't just accept the first solution. We explore the practice, ask questions and look for the route that connects knowledge, behaviour and experience.</span>
      </li>
    </ul>
  </div>
</section>
