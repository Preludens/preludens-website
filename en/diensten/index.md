      <h2>Dilemma Storytelling</h2>
      <p>
        Dilemma Stories are short, realistic scenarios in which people face difficult choices and immediately experience the consequences.
        That strengthens decisiveness, insight, and behaviour. Because there is no single right answer and every choice affects different KPIs,
        people learn to weigh options carefully, set priorities, and see how decisions play out in practice.
      </p>
